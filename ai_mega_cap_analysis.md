# US AI Tech Mega-Cap Financial Analysis

**Date**: July 19, 2026  
**Coverage**: NVDA, GOOGL, MSFT, AMZN, META, AMD, AVGO, TSM  
**Data Sources**: S&P Global Market Intelligence (via StockAnalysis.com), AKShare (East Money Financial Indicators), Yahoo Finance  
**Note**: All financial figures in USD unless otherwise noted. Market data as of July 17, 2026 close.

---

## Executive Summary

The eight US AI tech mega-caps represent a combined **~$20.7 trillion** in market capitalization. The group is characterized by:

- **Extraordinary growth bifurcation**: NVDA (+65% revenue YoY) and AVGO (+24%) lead, while mature hyperscalers (MSFT +15%, AMZN +12%, GOOGL +15%) show mid-teens growth
- **Massive AI infrastructure spending**: Combined trailing capex of **~$489B**, with AMZN alone spending $151B (20.3% of revenue)
- **Uneven profitability**: Gross margins range from NVDA's 74% to AMZN's 51%; net margins from NVDA's 63% to AMD's 13%
- **Strong balance sheets overall**: Net cash positions at NVDA ($40B), GOOGL ($31B), and TSM ($80B); net debt positions at MSFT (-$47B), AMZN (-$92B), AVGO (-$45B)
- **Modest shareholder returns**: Dividend yields of 0.25%-0.92% across dividend payers; buyback activity strongest at META and GOOGL
